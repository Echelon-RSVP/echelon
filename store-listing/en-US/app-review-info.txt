=== Notes (paste into Notes field) ===

Echelon is a social reputation app. Users sign in, browse a feed, message friends, discover people, and rate other users. Direct person ratings are limited to once every 24 hours per rated person across Lens/map, chat, calls, and video calls. Media ratings are independent and can be submitted once per media item.

Sign in steps:
1. Open the app and tap Sign in.
2. Choose email/password (not Sign in with Apple for the demo account).
3. Enter the credentials above. The account is pre-onboarded with friends, posts, messages, Lens enabled, and recent Lens locations.

How to test main features:
1. Feed: scroll posts, open a post, like or comment. Stories row at top (Momentum).
2. Messages: open a chat with Naomi or Iris, send a text message.
3. Profile: view portfolio, score graph, and settings.
4. Match: tap Match. The demo account already has height set. Tap a username/handle to open the profile, tap NEXT to pass, or tap the heart to like.
5. Rate others once every 24 hours: use either of these paths:
   - Match/Map path: tap the Map/Lens tab. Lens is on by default. The review account has Lens friends available on the map, including Iris Moon (@irismoon) and Marcus Bell (@marcusb), both seeded with Lens on and recent locations. Tap a user pin and tap Rate. After rating, the same user is blocked by the 24-hour cooldown.
   - Chat path: open Messages, select an existing chat, tap End Chat, then submit the required rating. The same 24-hour direct rating cooldown applies.
6. Media ratings: open Feed, single-tap a post to show empty stars, then tap a star. This rates the media item only and does not consume the direct person-rating cooldown.

Permissions (only requested when used):
- Camera/microphone: chat media and stories.
- Photo library: attach images.
- Location: optional, for Lens/map discovery of followed users who have Lens on.

Face data:
- The onboarding face scan collects only the selfie/profile photo the user submits.
- It is used only to create the profile avatar and request an automated image-analysis score suggestion for the initial in-app Echelon Score.
- Echelon does not collect Apple Face ID data, biometric face templates, faceprints, facial geometry, liveness maps, depth maps, or government ID data.
- The image may be sent to Google Gemini API solely to return the score suggestion. Hosting providers store the image only on Echelon-controlled infrastructure as the user's profile avatar. We do not sell, advertise with, or track users using face data.
- The avatar image is kept while the account is active or until replaced/deleted because it is the visible profile avatar. After account deletion, active-system copies are deleted or anonymised within 30 days; encrypted backups may persist up to 24 months for security/disaster recovery/legal integrity, then are purged. Server/security logs are typically retained up to 90 days.
- See Privacy Policy section "4A. Face data collection, use, sharing, storage, and retention".

iOS sign-in:
- The App Store iOS shell supports Sign in with Apple and email/password. Gmail is hidden in the iOS shell because Apple's ITMS-91061 notice requires GoogleSignIn/GTMAppAuth/GTMSessionFetcher privacy manifests that are not available in the current native Google plugin dependency chain. Gmail remains available on supported web/PWA environments. For fastest review access, please use the email/password demo account above.

Cookies/tracking:
- The app does not use cookies/local storage for advertising, data brokers, or cross-app/cross-site tracking.
- The cookie prompt is for strictly necessary storage and optional functional preferences only. Analytics are not currently deployed.
- See Cookie Policy sections 3, 4, 5, and 6.

User safety: users can block, mute, report posts, and delete their own messages.

The iOS app loads the web experience from https://echelon.rsvp/app/ inside a native shell. No special configuration is required.
