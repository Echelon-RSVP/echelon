=== Notes (paste into Notes field) ===

Echelon is a positive social community app. Users share posts and stories, message friends, discover people on the map, and grow an Echelon Score from media ratings and respectful participation.

Important compliance note (Guideline 1.2):
- Echelon does NOT support rating, ranking, or judging people.
- Users can rate posts, stories, and reels (media only).
- Onboarding captures a profile photo for the avatar only. No appearance analysis or attractiveness scoring.
- Match pass/Next does not reduce another user's score.
- Forced post-chat and post-call person ratings have been removed.

Demo account (email/password login):
- Email: review@echelon.rsvp
- Password: EchelonReview2026!

Launch / loading:
- The app should reach the sign-in screen within a few seconds on iPad. It does not auto-prompt Sign in with Apple on launch.
- If you see a brief loading spinner, wait up to 10 seconds; the sign-in screen will appear.

Sign in steps (iPhone or iPad):
1. Open the app and wait for the sign-in screen (should appear within a few seconds on launch).
2. Tap Sign in, then choose email/password (not Sign in with Apple for the demo account).
3. Enter the credentials above. The account is pre-onboarded with friends, posts, messages, Lens enabled, and recent Lens locations.

iPad launch note (Guideline 2.1):
- Build 21 fixes a launch hang on iPad caused by automatic Sign in with Apple on cold start.
- The app no longer prompts for Apple sign-in until the user taps Sign in with Apple.
- If you see a brief loading spinner on first launch, it should resolve to the sign-in screen within 10 seconds.

How to test main features (iPhone or iPad):
1. Feed: scroll posts. A purple "Rate posts & stories" banner explains media ratings. Single-tap any post to open stars, then tap a star.
2. Messages: tap Messages in the bottom dock. Open a chat and send a text message.
3. Profile: view portfolio, score graph, and settings.
4. Match: tap Match. Tap NEXT to pass or tap the heart to like. Mutual likes become chats. Passing does not affect scores.
5. Map: tap Map. Tap a friend pin for Profile, Follow, and Message. There is no Rate button for people.
6. Media ratings only: Echelon does not offer person ratings anywhere in the app.

Permissions (only requested when used):
- Camera/microphone: profile photo, chat media, and stories.
- Photo library: attach images.
- Location: optional, for Lens/map discovery of followed users who have Lens on.

Profile photo privacy:
- Onboarding stores a profile photo as the user's avatar only.
- Echelon does not send onboarding photos to AI appearance-analysis services.
- See Privacy Policy section "4A. Profile photo collection, use, sharing, storage, and retention".

iOS sign-in:
- The App Store iOS shell supports Sign in with Apple, email/password, and Gmail via browser OAuth with PKCE.

Cookies/tracking:
- The app does not use cookies/local storage for advertising, data brokers, or cross-app/cross-site tracking.

User safety: users can block, mute, report posts, and delete their own messages.

The iOS app loads the web experience from https://echelon.rsvp/app/ inside a native shell.
