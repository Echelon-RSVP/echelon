Echelon is a positive social community for sharing posts, stories, and real connections.

Post photos and videos, message friends, discover people on the map, and grow your Echelon Score through content quality and respectful participation.

• Feed, stories, and portfolio
• Direct messages with photos, voice, and video
• Discover friends nearby on the map
• Rate posts and stories (media only, not people)
• Match to meet people with mutual likes
• Friends, blocks, and privacy controls

How ratings work:
1. Feed: tap a post once to open stars, then tap a star to rate that media.
2. Echelon does not support rating, ranking, or judging people.

Echelon works on iPhone and iPad. Sign in with Apple, Gmail, or email to get started.
