Echelon is where your best self is finally measurable.

Build your reputation through real interactions: posts, stories, messages, and ratings from people who know you. Your score moves in real time and unlocks access across the app.

• Feed, stories, and portfolio
• Direct messages with photos, voice, and video
• Discover people nearby on the map
• Rate posts: tap any post once, then tap a star
• Rate people: open Map, tap a friend pin, then Rate (once per 24h when eligible)
• Friends, blocks, and privacy controls

How to rate in the app:
1. Feed: tap a post once to open stars, then tap a star to rate that media.
2. Map: open Map, tap a followed user on the map, then tap Rate.
3. Profile: open any profile and tap Rate.

Echelon works on iPhone and iPad. Sign in with Apple, Gmail, or email to get started.
