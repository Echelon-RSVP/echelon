Echelon is a social reputation app. Users sign in, browse a feed, message friends, discover people, and rate other users (limited to once per 24 hours per person when eligible).

Demo account (email login):
Email: review@echelon.rsvp

Sign in steps:
1. Open the app and tap Sign in.
2. Choose email/password (not Sign in with Apple for the demo account).
3. Enter the credentials above. The account is pre-onboarded with friends, posts, and messages.

How to test:
1. Feed: scroll posts, open a post, like or comment. Stories row at top (Momentum).
2. Messages: open a chat with Naomi or Iris, send a text message.
3. Profile: view portfolio, score graph, and settings.
4. Match: swipe profile cards.
5. Map/Discover: optional location permission for nearby users.

Permissions:
- Camera/microphone: chat media and stories.
- Photo library: attach images.
- Location: optional, for nearby discovery.

User safety: users can block, mute, report posts, and delete their own messages.

The iOS app loads https://echelon.rsvp/app/ inside a native shell.
